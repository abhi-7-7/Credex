## Copy Principles

1. **Numbers first** — every headline leads with a specific number when possible
2. **Verifiable** — every claim references a source the user can check
3. **No puffery** — "logic-based" and "verified against official pricing" are factual claims, not marketing language
4. **Second person** — "your team", "your spend", "you're currently" — never "our users" or "startups"
5. **Honest CTA sequencing** — Credex is introduced only after the value is shown, never before
